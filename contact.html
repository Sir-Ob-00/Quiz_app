Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest new questions?

Use the contact form above and select "Feature Request" as the subject. We're always looking for new questions to add to our database!

When will CSS and JavaScript questions be available?

We're working hard to add CSS and JavaScript categories. Follow our newsletter for updates on new features and content.

Can I reset my progress?

Yes! You can reset your progress using the "Reset All Progress" button on the main quiz page.

Is this app free to use?

Absolutely! Our quiz app is completely free and will remain so. We believe in making quality education accessible to everyone.
